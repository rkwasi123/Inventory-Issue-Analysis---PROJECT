💡 Insights & Recommendations

✅ Insight 1: Manual Adjustments are the primary driver of inventory changes (5.7%).
👉 Recommendation: Investigate why so many manual updates are required — could be due to staff training, system limitations, or inconsistent processes.

✅ Insight 2: Stockouts are very low (0.1%) — this is a positive sign.
👉 Recommendation: Maintain and monitor current stock level management — continue proactive monitoring.

✅ Insight 3: Top 3 Categories driving the most inventory activity:

  • Toys & Games
  • Office Supplies
  • Craft Supplies
👉 Recommendation: Prioritize inventory optimization and forecasting improvements for these categories, as they contribute the most activity.

✅ Insight 4: Steady activity in Pet Supplies and Jewelry.
👉 Recommendation: Monitor these categories for potential opportunities — may benefit from process automation or seasonal trend analysis.
